CHANNEL IQ

We know what you don't know.

CLIENT

Channel IQ is a Chicago-based start-up and the leading provider of online retail intelligence solutions to manufacturers, distributors and retailers. Two years ago, they released a client portal for users to view data, pull analytics, and communicate with retailers.

OPPORTUNITY

Channel IQ needed to discover why client activity was low in the new portal, even though every client had been trained on it.

SOLUTION

I was able to discover who, at a company, was using the services and how to improve the onboarding experiences and upkeep of an account.

PROJECT DETAILS

My Role: I was responsible for the research and documentation

Tools: Excel, Word, Powerpoint, Post-It Notes, Pen & Paper

Methods: User research, quantitative research, persona creation, and user flows.

RESEARCH

USER ANALYSIS

First, it was important to discover which of the clients were active in the Channel IQ client portal. I pulled a list of all the portal users who have been active in the last 60 days.

ONBOARDING ENABLEMENT FLOW

Next, I interviewed crucial members of the onboarding team to understand what the current process is and where each pain point occurs. With the information I gathered, I created an onboarding workflow.

PERSONAS

In order to create personas I interviewed clients, Account Managers, and reviewed qualitative data I had previously collected. I held a workshop with my fellow UX designer and 12 Account Managers to fill in any holes in the personas.
