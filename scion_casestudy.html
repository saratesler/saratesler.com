SCION

The end of spreadsheets.

CLIENT

As a freelancer, I joined the Bellwether Creation Company for a month to assist in the design of a dashboard for Scion, a leader in the student housing industry.

OPPORTUNITY

Scion's success in running dozens of student housing all over the country relied heavily on the diligence of managers on each property updating spreadsheets daily. Replacing limiting spreadsheets with a custom dashboard was a way for Scion to gain insights like never before.

SOLUTION

After various meetings with stakeholders, I was able to adjust the navigation of the dashboard prototype, rework features to maximize usability, and design key data entry pages as well as visual analytics for the dashboard.

PROJECT DETAILS

My Role: I was brought on as a UX designer. I participated in research, stakeholder interviews, ideation, sketching, and wireframes. I was responsible for making sure we tackled the project from a user friendly perspective.

Timeframe: One month
